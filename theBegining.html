The first few weeks

Getting set up, meeting the group and buckling down!

Let me start out by saying that I have taken this class before but I didn't pass because of what was going on around and because of the dificulty of the class itself. This is (hopefully) my last sememster so the pressure was on to keep up with the class.

When the class first started, I found myself eager to get a head start. I have taken this class before so it should theoretically give me an advantage. I believe that it was the start of the second week that we finally got into our groups and we could begin working on our schedule. Me and another student were communicating in the wrong place, so I already started off on the wrong foot and I was late to our first group meeting.

The First Meeting

This meeting was all about getting to know each other and getting the schedule together in accordance with the assignment:

Indicate the following in your plans: Which topics you and your group will learn that week and who will be teaching them.”
